WHO WE ARE

At Kasama, we are more than just a restaurant. Our name, “Kasama,” meaning “companion” in Tagalog, reflects our commitment to welcoming you as a cherished companion on an unforgettable adventure. With a deep passion for Filipino cuisine, we take pride in showcasing its rich and diverse flavors. We invite you to join us on this culinary voyage and become part of our Kasama family.
